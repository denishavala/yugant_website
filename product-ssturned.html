SS Turned Components

We manufacture premium stainless steel (SS) CNC machined turned parts, pins, shafts, fittings, and customized adapters. Excellent for corrosion-resistant demands in medical instruments, food-processing systems, chemical mixers, and aerospace equipment, our SS parts hold strict tolerances.

Material Grades SS 304, SS 316, SS 316L, SS 303 (Free-machining grade)
Dimensional Tolerance Holds strict tolerances down to ±0.01mm
Machining Capacity Diameter range from 2mm to 150mm on CNC turning centers
Surface Cleanliness Passivated, acid pickling, electro-polished, satin finish
Request Quote Download Brochure

Key Features

  • CNC machined surface roughness values down to Ra 0.4
  • Grade 316 stainless composition guarantees acid proofing
  • Passivation chemical baths prevent microscopic rust spots
  • Precise roundness checks ensure high-speed rotational balancing

Applications

  • Hygienic connector couplings inside food processing lines
  • High-durability pump impeller shafts in chemical industries
  • Corrosion-free structural pins in marine rigging brackets
  • Medical equipment fluid transfer valves control spindles

Advantages

  • Extreme tensile durability under substantial mechanical wear
  • Alloy certificates trace material source batches strictly
  • Excellent tolerance limits eliminate friction heat buildup
  • High temperature resistance supports high-pressure steam cleaning
