Auditing Steps

Our Strict Inspection Process

Quality inspection occurs at three defined control gates during our manufacturing cycle.

Gate 1: Incoming Rod Audit
Raw Material Verification

We verify the chemical composition of incoming brass rods under spectrometer testing. Rods containing radioactive contamination or incorrect copper ratios are rejected.

Gate 2: In-Process CNC Audit
First Article Dimensional Check

Once tooling is set, initial parts are tested against CAD blueprints. Production continues only after inspectors confirm coordinates using digital micrometers.

Gate 3: Pre-Dispatch Audit
Visual & Plating Checks

Finished components undergo thread ring gauge audits and coating thickness checks. Verified parts are then vacuum sealed in anti-tarnish plastic sleeves.
