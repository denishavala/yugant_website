Fire & Safety Components

Our fire safety catalog contains high-integrity components machined to support critical automatic sprinkler grids and pressure valve assemblies. By using hot press forging and high-speed CNC centers, we eliminate structural leakage risks, ensuring rapid flow response under extreme thermal conditions.

Product Varieties Sprinkler Deflector Frames, Fire Nozzle Bushings, Adapter Couplings
Pressure Rating Supports up to 25 Bar operating pressure
Material Standard CW617N, CZ122, Lead-Free Brass (optional)
Thread Compliance NPT, BSPT, NPSM (strictly calibrated)
Request Quote Download Brochure

Key Features

  • Hot-pressed forge bodies eliminate internal air bubbles
  • 100% pneumatic leak test on all valve bodies
  • Finely machined deflector plate structures for water spray balance
  • Strict thread compliance guarantees leak-free high-pressure connection

Applications

  • Commercial fire sprinkler nozzle frames installation
  • Pressure-reducing valves in industrial fire cabinets
  • Fire hydrant adapters and custom landing valves
  • High-rise piping system manifold couplings

Advantages

  • Zero compromise on material strength under extreme heat
  • UL/FM standards compliant testing on request
  • Available in lead-free brass for drinking water links
  • Outstanding surface finishes prevent calcium sediment buildup
