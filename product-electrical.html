Electrical & Electronics

We manufacture a complete line of brass neutral links, earth bars, terminal block connectors, switch parts, and electrical plug pins. Machined from high-conductivity free-cutting brass, these parts ensure maximum contact stability and safe current distribution inside power enclosures.

Category Varieties Neutral Links, Earth Terminal Bars, Meter Connector Terminals, Switchgear Pins
Electrical Conductivity Excellent electrical and thermal conductivity characteristics
Material Standard CZ121, CW614N, High-Purity Copper CDA-110 (optional)
Plating / Coating Electro-Tin Coated (prevents copper migration), Nickel plated, Clean Brass
Request Quote Download Brochure

Key Features

  • High brass alloy purity yields low contact resistance
  • Clean threaded screws prevent wire shearing during assembly
  • Available electro-tin finish stops atmospheric corrosion
  • Sturdy cross-sections distribute high electrical loads safely

Applications

  • Earth ground termination blocks inside panel board boxes
  • Custom brass connection blocks in industrial power meters
  • High-current brass connector pins inside commercial plug connections
  • Heavy-duty switchgear and distribution control hardware panels

Advantages

  • Strict tolerance checks assure correct fits in busbar grooves
  • Substantial mechanical strength prevents thread stripping
  • Custom spacing configurations built to client blueprints
  • Excellent heating cycle limits under intensive load conditions
