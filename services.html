Capabilities

Our Services

Providing end-to-end custom metal fabrication, CNC tooling design, surface plating, and global logistics options.

Custom CAD/CAM Design

We convert your engineering ideas, sketches, and PDF drawings into precise 3D models. Our engineers optimize tooling setups to ensure perfect machine toolpaths before manufacturing begins.

Learn More

CNC & VMC Machining

Equipped with multi-axis CNC machine lines, we achieve highly intricate cuts, complex slots, and cylindrical contours, holding tolerances down to ±0.01mm across long production runs.

Learn More

Automatic Lathe Turning

For high-volume standard turned components, our automated single-spindle lathe arrays produce high quantities of inserts, electrical pins, and studs with maximum raw materials utilization.

Learn More

Hot Press Forging

We operate hot forging presses for structural components like valves and hose adapters. Forging aligns the metallic grains, guaranteeing heavy structural integrity and zero internal air porosity.

Learn More

Industrial Threading

We machine threads to international standards, including Metric, NPT, BSP, and BSPT. Thread accuracy is tested at every step using go/no-go plug and ring gauges.

Learn More

Finishing & Plating

We offer multiple finish options, including electroplated nickel, bright chrome plating, zinc passivation, and tin coating. Finished parts are packed in vacuum seal bags to prevent oxidation.

Learn More
