About Yugant Engineering

Discover our manufacturing capabilities, quality-centered mission, and state-of-the-art facility structure.

Our History

Decades of Engineering Passion

Established in 2011, Yugant Engineering began as a small manufacturing unit for simple brass fittings. Through constant investment in automatic technology, computerized tooling configurations, and personnel training, we grew into a global export partner.

Today, our facility serves multi-national OEMs in the HVAC, fire suppression, electronics, and automotive industries across 5 continents, providing fully compliant and specialized metallic components.

2011
Founding Year

Started production in Jamnagar, serving regional hardware buyers.

2017
CNC Shop Expansion

Upgraded our facility and integrated digital profile projector QC desks.

2022
Global Exports Launch

Acquired logistics networks and began container shipment dispatches to EU and US buyers.

Our Mission

To supply premium machined components matching client blueprints with dimensional consistency, optimizing materials utilization to offer highly competitive pricing parameters and eco-friendly dispatches.

Our Vision

To lead as a premier global manufacturing facility for brass, copper, and stainless steel hardware parts, known for adopting automation and maintaining zero-defect standards on high-volume shipments.
